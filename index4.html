Technology Transfer Team (TTT)

Roles and Responsibilities

  • To establish required infrastructure and services for supporting intellectual property
  • To make an attempt to innovative ideas from the faculty, staff and students through licenses, new and expanding ideas and promoting to the society.
  • To provide guidance to intra-mural and extra-mural scientists on all technology transfer matters
  • To build and promoting communication, conflict resolution, and emotional intelligence and many other related transferable skills to the student community.
  • To conduct a competitive analysis to understand the value or advantage of an invention over existing technologies in the invention and research areas.
  • To negotiate commercial contracts with relevant industry sectors and ideally relate it to the licensing of software products and services.

Vision


To develop a technology transfer infrastructure that supports successful commercialization of innovative ideas by faculty, staff and students


Mission


To enable and guide collaborations, invention development and licensing to advance present day discoveries into forecasting welfare of the society.
